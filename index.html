About

Let me introduce myself.

As a visual designer with six years of experience in graphic design, I have gained proficiency in industry-standard tools such as Photoshop, InDesign, and Illustrator during my full-time employment and internship. Additionally, I have utilized prototyping tools like Figma and Adobe XD to develop compelling and user-friendly designs.

Since I was young, I have always had a passion for design and artwork. My fascination with the relationship between type, layout, color, and space and how it impacts the overall mood and message led me to pursue a career in visual design.

With a creative and inquisitive mindset, I approach each project with a focus on both aesthetic appeal and user experience. Collaborating with project stakeholders and utilizing design thinking methodologies, I strive to create visually engaging and intuitive designs that effectively communicate the intended message.

I am excited to continue pushing the boundaries of design and explore new technologies and techniques to enhance my skills and craft.
