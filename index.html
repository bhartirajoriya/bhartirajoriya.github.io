More of my credentials.

I was born and raised in India. I moved to San Francisco around 6 years back. In my past life, I was working in digital marketing field. I have always had a passion for design & artwork. Ever since, I remember, I’m fascinated with the way type, layout, color & space relate to information and how it impacts the mood. This curiosity led me to pursue a career in Visual design. I’m constantly seeking design inspiration & new experiences.  I am intrigued by how aesthetics and style change with technology and culture or how people take principles of the past and apply them to create something unique and modern.
